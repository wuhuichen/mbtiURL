About MBTI Insight

MBTI Insight is a personality assessment app based on the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI). Our app helps users understand their personality type through a series of carefully designed questions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I reset my test progress?

A: You can start a new test at any time by going to the Test tab and selecting "Start New Test".

Q: Can I save my test results?

A: Yes, your test results are automatically saved in the app. You can view your history in the Results section.

Q: Is my data private?

A: Yes, all your test data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or store any personal information.

Q: How accurate is the test?

A: Our test is designed based on MBTI principles, but it should be used as a self-discovery tool rather than a professional psychological assessment.

Known Issues

  • Some users may experience delayed animations on older devices
  • The app requires iOS 15.0 or later
